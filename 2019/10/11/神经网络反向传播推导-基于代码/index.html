神经网络反向传播推导

前言：最近在看《深度学习之PyTorch实战计算机视觉》这本书，里边第6章中《搭建一个简单的神经网络》中有一部分代码看不懂，经过与学长的探讨，进行了手工推导，明白了为何代码是这样写的。

ps: 为了debug时候的简便，改动了x,y,w1,w2的Tensor形状

2. 推导过程

graph TD
x--w1--> h1
h1--w2-->y_pred

loss表达式如下：

y^=x×w1×w2loss=argmin(∑(y^−y)2)\hat{y}=x\times w_1 \times w_2 \\ loss = argmin(\sum{(\hat{y}-y)^2}) y^​=x×w1​×w2​loss=argmin(∑(y^​−y)2)

第一步：

​ grad_y_pred = 2*(y_pred-y)

∂loss∂y^=2(y^−y)\frac{\partial loss}{\partial \hat{y}}=2(\hat{y}-y) ∂y^​∂loss​=2(y^​−y)

第二步：

​ grad_w2 = h1.t().mm(grad_y_pred)

∂loss∂w2=∂loss∂y^×∂y^∂w2∂y^∂w2=∂h1w2∂w2=h1T∂loss∂w2=∂loss∂y^×∂y^∂w2=2×(y^−y)×h1T\frac{\partial loss}{\partial w_2} = \frac{\partial loss}{\partial \hat{y}}\times \frac{\partial \hat{y}}{\partial w_2} \\ \frac{\partial \hat{y}}{\partial w_2} = \frac{\partial h_1w_2}{\partial w_2} = h_1^{T} \\ \frac{\partial loss}{\partial w_2} = \frac{\partial loss}{\partial \hat{y}}\times \frac{\partial \hat{y}}{\partial w_2} = 2\times(\hat{y}-y)\times h_1^{T} ∂w2​∂loss​=∂y^​∂loss​×∂w2​∂y^​​∂w2​∂y^​​=∂w2​∂h1​w2​​=h1T​∂w2​∂loss​=∂y^​∂loss​×∂w2​∂y^​​=2×(y^​−y)×h1T​

与代码相符

第三步：

grad_h = grad_y_pred.clone()
grad_h = grad_h.mm(w2.t())

∂loss∂h1=∂loss∂y^×∂y^∂h1=2×(y^−y)×w2T\frac{\partial loss}{\partial h_1}=\frac{\partial loss}{\partial \hat{y}}\times \frac{\partial \hat{y}}{\partial h_1} = 2\times (\hat{y}-y)\times w_2^{T} ∂h1​∂loss​=∂y^​∂loss​×∂h1​∂y^​​=2×(y^​−y)×w2T​

第四步：

​ grad_w1 = x.t().mm(grad_h)

∂loss∂w1=∂loss∂y^×∂y^∂w1=2×(y^−y)×w2T×xT\frac{\partial loss}{\partial w_1}=\frac{\partial loss}{\partial \hat{y}}\times \frac{\partial \hat{y}}{\partial w_1}=2\times(\hat{y}-y)\times w_2^{T}\times x^{T} ∂w1​∂loss​=∂y^​∂loss​×∂w1​∂y^​​=2×(y^​−y)×w2T​×xT

3. 用到的公式

矩阵求导：

∂wx∂x=wT\frac{\partial wx}{\partial x}=w^{T} ∂x∂wx​=wT
